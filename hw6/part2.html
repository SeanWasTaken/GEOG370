Classification 2: Equal Interval

This ratio is categorized using equal intervals, where the attributes are divided into equal sized ranges. The advantage of this method is the creation of an easy to interpret legend, however it causes most data values to fall into one or two classes. This disadvantage of equal intervals can be seen on this map, as most counties are a light shade of green, with one county being very dark green.
